# SchoolOS Research Workspace

This directory is the research laboratory of the SchoolOS project.

It contains ideas that are **not yet accepted** as part of the official documentation.

The purpose of this workspace is to explore, challenge and refine ideas before they become part of the project's philosophy, architecture or product.

---

## Workflow

Every idea follows the same lifecycle:

Research

↓

Discussion

↓

Counterexamples

↓

Revision

↓

Acceptance

↓

Documentation

↓

Architecture

↓

Implementation

---

## Principles

- Nothing here is considered final.
- Every hypothesis should be challenged.
- Every important claim should have supporting arguments.
- Counterexamples are encouraged.
- Only mature ideas move into the `docs/` directory.

## Relationship with `/docs`

- `research/` contains hypotheses and ongoing discussions.
- `docs/` contains accepted and stable knowledge.

Research is the laboratory.

Documentation is the library.
