---

# Core Philosophy

## Human Development

Development is a natural and lifelong process.

Success is one possible milestone within development.

It is not the purpose of development.

---

## Evidence

Development cannot be measured directly.

Development can only be understood through meaningful evidence accumulated over time.

Observations are not evidence.

Evidence emerges through meaningful, corroborated observations collected across different contexts and time.

Every important insight generated by SchoolOS must be traceable back to supporting evidence.

---

## School

Schools are living learning communities.

SchoolOS exists to strengthen their capacity to learn, remember and continuously improve.

---

## Institutional Memory

Knowledge should never disappear when people leave.

Institutional memory is one of the school's most valuable assets.

SchoolOS exists to preserve and strengthen that memory.
