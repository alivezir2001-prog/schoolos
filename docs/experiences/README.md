# Purpose

The Experiences directory defines how people interact with SchoolOS.

Rather than organizing the platform around software modules, SchoolOS is organized around educational experiences.

Each experience represents the needs, responsibilities and workflows of a specific user.

Together, these experiences create a connected educational ecosystem.

---

# Vision

SchoolOS is designed around people, not features.

Every screen, workflow and AI capability exists to improve someone's educational experience.

Technology is never the goal.

Meaningful educational experiences are.

---

# Experience Model

SchoolOS currently defines five primary educational experiences.

```text
                    SchoolOS

                       │

        ┌──────────────┼──────────────┐

        │              │              │

 Observation      Teacher       Learner

 Experience      Experience    Experience

        │              │              │

        └──────────────┼──────────────┘

                       │

          Parent Experience

                       │

          Administrator Experience
```

Although each experience serves different users, they all contribute to the same educational journey.

---

# Observation Experience

Observation is the foundation of SchoolOS.

Teachers capture meaningful educational observations.

These observations become educational evidence.

Evidence supports developmental understanding.

Everything begins with observation.

---

# Teacher Experience

The Teacher Experience supports educators throughout their daily work.

Its goal is to reduce administrative burden while increasing educational understanding.

Teachers observe.

Reflect.

Support.

Guide.

SchoolOS assists throughout this process.

---

# Learner Experience

The Learner Experience preserves the educational story of every learner.

It brings together observations, evidence, development, educational continuity and AI-supported educational understanding.

The learner remains at the center of the platform.

---

# Parent Experience

The Parent Experience helps families understand and support their child's educational journey.

Parents receive meaningful educational information while respecting professional educational boundaries and privacy.

---

# Administrator Experience

The Administrator Experience helps school leaders support educational quality, collaboration and institutional learning.

Its purpose is not only administration but also educational leadership.

---

# Shared Principles

Every educational experience follows the same principles.

- Education before administration.
- Understanding before evaluation.
- Evidence before assumptions.
- Growth before labels.
- Privacy before convenience.
- Professional judgement before automation.
