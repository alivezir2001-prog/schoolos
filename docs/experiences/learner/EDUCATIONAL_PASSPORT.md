# Purpose

The Educational Passport preserves educational continuity throughout a learner's educational journey.

Its purpose is to ensure that educational understanding is not lost when teachers, classes, academic years or schools change.

Rather than transferring records, the Educational Passport transfers educational understanding.

It helps educators begin with context instead of starting from zero.

---

# Vision

Every learner carries an educational story.

Schools contribute to that story.

Teachers contribute to that story.

The Educational Passport ensures that the story continues.

Educational continuity is not about preserving data.

It is about preserving understanding.

---

# Educational Role

The Educational Passport represents the learner's educational identity beyond the boundaries of a single classroom or school.

It provides educators with a concise, trustworthy and evidence-supported educational summary that enables a smooth educational transition.

Its purpose is not to evaluate learners.

Its purpose is to support them.

---

# Core Principles

The Educational Passport follows several fundamental principles.

## Educational Continuity

Learning should continue even when educational environments change.

---

## Educational Understanding

The passport transfers educational understanding rather than administrative records.

---

## Evidence-Based

Every meaningful statement within the passport must be supported by educational evidence.

---

## Explainable

Every AI-generated conclusion must be traceable to educational evidence.

---

## Privacy

The passport always respects authorization, privacy and legal requirements.

Only appropriate educational information should be shared.

---

## Growth

The passport is never permanent.

It evolves as the learner grows.

Recent educational evidence always carries greater importance than outdated assumptions.

---

# Core Components

The Educational Passport may include the following sections.

## Educational Identity

A concise educational introduction to the learner.

---

## AI Teacher Brief

A one-minute educational summary prepared for teachers.

---

## Learning Preferences

How the learner most effectively engages with learning.

---

## Educational Strengths

Educational areas where the learner consistently demonstrates growth.

---

## Support Strategies

Educational practices that have successfully supported the learner.

---

## Educational Milestones

Significant moments within the learner's educational journey.

---

## Learning History

A high-level educational timeline describing meaningful learning experiences.

---

## Important Educational Alerts

Role-based educational information that may require attention.

Examples include:

- Learning accommodations
- Important health considerations
- Communication preferences

Only authorized information is included.

---

# What the Passport Does Not Include

The Educational Passport is not an archive.

It should not include:

- Disciplinary history
- Confidential administrative correspondence
- Subjective personal opinions
- Unsupported assumptions
- Unnecessary personal information

The passport exists to support learning, not to define the learner.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence assists by:

- Generating educational summaries
- Identifying meaningful educational patterns
- Highlighting significant educational changes
- Preparing transition briefs
- Supporting educational continuity

Artificial Intelligence never replaces professional educational judgement.

Teachers remain responsible for educational decisions.

---

# Educational Transition

When a learner changes teachers or schools, the Educational Passport supports educational continuity through a concise transition summary.

The transition focuses on:

- Educational strengths
- Successful support strategies
- Learning preferences
- Current developmental priorities

The transition prepares educators to support learning from the very first day.

---

# Consent and Ownership

The Educational Passport belongs to the learner.

Schools contribute educational knowledge.

Teachers contribute professional observations.

Artificial Intelligence organizes educational understanding.

No single institution owns the learner's story.

Educational information should only be shared in accordance with applicable laws, organizational policies, and appropriate authorization.

---

# Right to Growth

The Educational Passport must never permanently define a learner.

Educational understanding should always remain open to change.

Learners grow.

Circumstances change.

New educational experiences may replace previous interpretations.

The passport exists to preserve continuity, never to limit potential.
