# Purpose

The Learner Profile is the central educational representation of a learner within SchoolOS.

It is designed to help educators understand learners through meaningful educational context rather than isolated records, grades or administrative information.

Rather than answering **"What happened?"**, the Learner Profile seeks to answer:

**"Who is this learner becoming?"**

---

# Vision

Every learner deserves to be understood before being evaluated.

The Learner Profile provides teachers with meaningful educational understanding that supports better educational decisions, stronger relationships and educational continuity.

It is not a report.

It is not a transcript.

It is not a collection of data.

It is the educational understanding of a learner.

---

# Educational Role

The Learner Profile serves as the primary educational interface between teachers and learners.

It transforms educational information into meaningful understanding.

Its purpose is to reduce the time required for teachers to understand a learner while preserving the learner's educational story across time.

---

# Design Goals

The Learner Profile should:

- Present understanding before information.
- Preserve educational continuity.
- Support professional judgement.
- Encourage learner growth.
- Explain educational patterns.
- Protect learner privacy.
- Adapt as the learner develops.

---

# Core Components

The Learner Profile is composed of several interconnected sections.

## Learner Identity

Basic learner information and educational identity.

---

## Educational Summary

An AI-assisted educational summary generated from authorized educational evidence.

This summary provides teachers with a concise overview of the learner within approximately one minute.

---

## Educational Context

Relevant educational information that helps teachers better understand the learner.

Examples include:

- Learning supports
- Previous educational experiences
- Health considerations (role-based)
- Family context (role-based)
- Educational accommodations

Educational context exists to support learning, never to create prejudice.

---

## Observation Timeline

A chronological history of meaningful educational observations and events.

The timeline preserves the learner's educational story.

---

## Educational Evidence

Evidence generated from meaningful observation patterns.

Evidence represents educational understanding rather than isolated observations.

---

## Development

An evolving interpretation of learner growth supported by educational evidence.

Development reflects progress over time rather than static evaluation.

---

## Portfolio

A living collection of meaningful educational experiences, achievements and milestones.

The portfolio celebrates growth rather than performance alone.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence supports the Learner Profile by:

- summarizing educational information,
- identifying meaningful patterns,
- explaining educational evidence,
- highlighting significant changes,
- generating teacher briefs.

Artificial Intelligence never replaces professional educational judgement.

Teachers remain responsible for educational decisions.

---

# Explainability

Every AI-generated insight must be explainable.

Teachers should always be able to understand:

- what evidence supports an insight,
- why the insight was generated,
- how confident the system is,
- which educational observations contributed.

Educational understanding should never become a black box.

---

# Privacy

Different users require different educational information.

The Learner Profile respects role-based authorization.

Only information that is educationally relevant and authorized should be visible to each user.

Artificial Intelligence must never expose information that the current user is not authorized to access.

---

# Educational Continuity

The Learner Profile preserves educational continuity across teachers, academic years and, where legally and ethically appropriate, school transitions.

Every teacher contributes another chapter.

The learner's story continues.

---

# Future Vision

The Learner Profile is designed as a living educational representation.

As SchoolOS evolves, the profile will continue to integrate new educational evidence, richer AI capabilities and deeper educational understanding while remaining faithful to its educational philosophy.

The profile grows because the learner grows.

---

# SchoolOS Promise

The Learner Profile exists to help teachers understand learners, not to define them.

Every learner can grow.

Every observation adds context.

Every teacher contributes understanding.

Every school preserves continuity.

The story always belongs to the learner.
