# Purpose

The AI Teacher Brief provides educators with a concise, evidence-based educational summary of a learner.

Its purpose is to help teachers understand a learner quickly, accurately and responsibly before making educational decisions.

Rather than replacing professional judgement, the AI Teacher Brief provides meaningful educational context.

The goal is simple:

A teacher should be able to understand the educational profile of a learner within one minute.

---

# Vision

Teachers should spend their time teaching learners, not rediscovering them.

The AI Teacher Brief transforms educational observations, evidence and context into a concise educational summary that supports better teaching from the very first day.

Educational understanding should be available immediately.

Trust should be built over time.

---

# Educational Role

The AI Teacher Brief serves as the first educational overview presented within the Learner Profile.

It summarizes meaningful educational information while encouraging teachers to explore the learner's complete educational story.

The brief introduces understanding.

The Learner Profile provides depth.

---

# Core Principles

The AI Teacher Brief follows several fundamental principles.

## Evidence Before Opinion

Every statement must be supported by educational evidence.

The system never generates unsupported conclusions.

---

## Explainable

Every educational insight must be traceable to the observations and evidence that support it.

Teachers should always understand why a conclusion exists.

---

## Role-Based

Different educators require different information.

The brief respects authorization and only includes information appropriate for the current user's role.

---

## Current

Recent educational evidence should carry greater importance than outdated information.

The brief continuously evolves as learners grow.

---

## Concise

The brief should be readable within approximately one minute.

Its purpose is orientation, not comprehensive analysis.

---

# Typical Contents

An AI Teacher Brief may include:

- Educational summary
- Learning preferences
- Educational strengths
- Areas currently requiring support
- Significant recent developments
- Successful educational strategies
- Important educational alerts (role-based)

The exact contents depend on the educator's role and authorization.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence creates the Teacher Brief by analysing:

- Teacher observations
- Educational evidence
- Development history
- Observation patterns
- Learning history
- Educational context
- Other authorized educational information

The AI identifies patterns.

Teachers interpret them.

---

# Explainability

Every statement within the AI Teacher Brief should answer the following questions:

- What educational evidence supports this statement?
- Which observations contributed?
- How recent is the evidence?
- How confident is the interpretation?

Educational understanding should always remain transparent.

---

# Confidence

Every significant educational insight should include an internal confidence assessment.

Confidence increases when:

- Multiple educators report similar observations.
- Observations remain consistent over time.
- Evidence appears across different educational contexts.

Low-confidence insights should never be presented as facts.

---

# Educational Ethics

The AI Teacher Brief exists to support learners.

It must never:

- Label learners.
- Predict fixed outcomes.
- Replace professional judgement.
- Present assumptions as facts.

Educational understanding should always remain open to growth.

---

The philosophy remains unchanged.

AI supports teachers.

Teachers support learners.
