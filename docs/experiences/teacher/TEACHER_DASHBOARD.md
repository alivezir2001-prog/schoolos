# Purpose

The Teacher Dashboard is the primary workspace of every teacher in SchoolOS.

Its purpose is not to display information.

Its purpose is to help teachers decide where their attention is needed today.

The dashboard transforms educational data into meaningful daily guidance.

Teachers should begin every day with educational understanding rather than administrative tasks.

---

# Vision

Teachers make hundreds of educational decisions every day.

SchoolOS helps teachers focus on the decisions that matter most.

Rather than presenting large amounts of information, the Teacher Dashboard highlights what deserves attention today.

---

# Educational Role

The Teacher Dashboard is the starting point of the teacher's daily workflow.

It connects:

- Today's schedule
- Learner understanding
- Educational observations
- AI-generated insights
- Educational continuity

Everything begins here.

# Educational Questions

The Teacher Dashboard should answer five questions every morning.

## 1. What does my day look like?

Today's lessons.

Today's classes.

Today's responsibilities.

---

## 2. Which learners need my attention today?

Learners requiring additional support.

New learners.

Learners showing meaningful changes.

Observation opportunities.

---

## 3. What has changed since yesterday?

Recent observations.

New educational evidence.

Important learner developments.

New AI insights.

---

## 4. What should I observe today?

Suggested observation opportunities.

Learners with limited recent observations.

Suggested follow-up observations.

---

## 5. How can I better support my learners today?

AI-generated educational recommendations.

Suggested teaching strategies.

Class-level educational insights.

# Dashboard Components

## Welcome

A simple greeting with today's educational overview.

Example:

- Today's lessons
- Number of learners
- General AI summary

---

## Today's Schedule

Chronological view of today's timetable.

Every lesson provides direct access to the corresponding class.

---

## Attention Center

The most important section of the dashboard.

Highlights learners requiring attention based on educational evidence.

Possible reasons include:

- Significant positive development
- Reduced participation
- New learner
- Suggested observation
- Important educational alert

Attention is educational.

Never disciplinary.

---

## My Classes

Overview of today's classes.

Each class displays:

- Number of learners
- Observation activity
- Educational highlights
- Quick access

---

## Observation Opportunities

Learners who may benefit from additional observations.

The purpose is to encourage educational reflection rather than enforce data entry.

---

## AI Educational Insights

AI-generated summaries based on recent educational evidence.

Insights may be:

- Individual
- Class-level
- Weekly
- Monthly

Every insight must be explainable.

---

# Design Principles

The Teacher Dashboard should:

- Show priorities before information.
- Support attention rather than administration.
- Reduce cognitive load.
- Encourage meaningful observations.
- Promote educational reflection.
- Respect professional judgement.
- Explain every AI recommendation.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

AI supports the Teacher Dashboard by:

- identifying learners who may need attention,
- summarizing recent educational changes,
- recommending observation opportunities,
- highlighting educational patterns,
- generating educational insights.

AI supports teachers.

Teachers make decisions.
