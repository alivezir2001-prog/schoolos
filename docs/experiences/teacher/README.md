# Purpose

The Teacher Experience defines how educators interact with SchoolOS throughout their daily educational work.

Rather than focusing on administrative tasks, the Teacher Experience is designed to help teachers understand learners, make informed educational decisions and preserve educational continuity.

Every interaction within SchoolOS should ultimately support better teaching.

---

# Vision

Teachers should spend their time supporting learners rather than managing software.

SchoolOS exists to reduce administrative burden while increasing educational understanding.

Technology should remain invisible.

Educational relationships should remain visible.

---

# Educational Role

Teachers are the primary contributors to the SchoolOS Educational Intelligence Platform.

Every meaningful educational observation, reflection and interaction enriches the learner's educational story.

The Teacher Experience transforms daily educational practice into long-term educational understanding.

---

# Educational Goals

The Teacher Experience aims to:

- Support informed educational decision-making.
- Encourage meaningful educational observations.
- Reduce administrative workload.
- Preserve institutional educational memory.
- Promote educational continuity.
- Support collaboration among educators.
- Strengthen reflective teaching practices.

---

# Core Workflow

The Teacher Experience follows a continuous educational cycle.

```text
Teacher Login

        ↓

Teacher Dashboard

        ↓

Today's Classes

        ↓

Class Overview

        ↓

Learner Card

        ↓

AI Teacher Brief

        ↓

Learner Profile

        ↓

Observation Editor

        ↓

Educational Evidence

        ↓

Development

        ↓

Teacher Dashboard
```

Every observation strengthens educational understanding.

Every cycle contributes to the learner's story.

---

# Core Components

## Teacher Dashboard

The starting point of every school day.

Provides priorities, schedules and educational insights.

---

## Class Overview

An educational overview of the entire class.

Highlights learners, classroom patterns and observation opportunities.

---

## Learner Card

A concise educational snapshot of an individual learner.

Provides quick access to the Learner Profile and AI Teacher Brief.

---

## Learner Profile

A comprehensive educational understanding of the learner.

Supports long-term educational decision-making.

---

## AI Teacher Brief

A one-minute educational summary generated from educational evidence.

Helps teachers quickly understand the learner before teaching.

---

## Observation Editor

Allows teachers to record meaningful educational observations.

Observation is the foundation of educational understanding within SchoolOS.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence supports teachers by:

- summarizing educational information,
- identifying meaningful educational patterns,
- suggesting observation opportunities,
- highlighting important learner changes,
- generating educational insights.

Artificial Intelligence supports teachers.

Teachers remain responsible for educational decisions.

---

# Design Principles

The Teacher Experience should always:

- Reduce cognitive load.
- Minimize unnecessary administration.
- Encourage meaningful observations.
- Present educational priorities before data.
- Explain every AI-generated insight.
- Respect teacher autonomy.
- Protect learner privacy.

---

# Educational Philosophy

The Teacher Experience is built upon four educational actions.

Observe.

Understand.

Support.

Grow.

Every feature within SchoolOS should strengthen at least one of these actions.
