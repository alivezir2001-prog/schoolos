# Purpose

The Learner Card provides teachers with an immediate educational snapshot of a learner.

Its purpose is to support quick educational decisions during daily classroom interactions without requiring teachers to open the full Learner Profile.

The Learner Card bridges classroom action and long-term educational understanding.

---

# Vision

Teachers often need immediate educational context.

During a lesson there is rarely time to read a complete learner profile.

The Learner Card provides the most relevant educational information within seconds while allowing seamless access to deeper educational understanding when needed.

---

# Educational Role

The Learner Card serves as the teacher's primary entry point to an individual learner.

It connects classroom interactions with educational intelligence.

It answers the question:

> **"What do I need to know about this learner right now?"**

---

AI-generated educational summary.

---

# Core Components

## Learner Identity

Displays:

- Name
- Photograph
- Class
- School Number

The learner always comes first.

---

## AI Snapshot

A concise educational summary.

Readable within approximately 20–30 seconds.

Focused on today's educational context.

---

## Recent Activity

Shows:

- Latest observation
- Recent evidence
- Significant educational changes

Teachers immediately understand what has changed.

---

## Attention Items

Educational items requiring attention.

Examples:

- Observation recommended
- Positive development
- New learner
- Follow-up suggested
- Important educational consideration

The goal is educational support, never labeling.

---

## Quick Actions

Teachers can immediately:

- Write Observation
- Open Learner Profile
- View Timeline
- View Portfolio

The Learner Card minimizes unnecessary navigation.

---

# Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence supports the Learner Card by:

- summarizing current educational context,
- identifying meaningful educational changes,
- highlighting observation opportunities,
- suggesting educational priorities.

AI supports educational awareness.

Teachers remain responsible for educational decisions.

---

# Design Principles

The Learner Card should:

- Load instantly.
- Be readable within 30 seconds.
- Present educational understanding before data.
- Encourage meaningful observation.
- Explain AI-generated insights.
- Support classroom decisions.
- Reduce unnecessary navigation.
