# ADR-017

## Title

Context Enriches Observations But Never Defines Them

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Date

2026-07-13

---

## Context

Observations may initially lack complete educational context.

Preventing observations until context is complete risks losing valuable educational reality.

---

## Decision

Observations remain independent.

Educational context enriches observations but never determines whether an observation may be recorded.

Missing context must never prevent capturing educational reality.

---

## Rationale

Reality should always be preserved first.

Educational context can be added later.

Lost observations cannot be recovered.

---

## Consequences

Observation Engine remains lightweight and responsive.

Context Model evolves independently.

---

## Alternatives Considered

### Require complete context before recording observations.

Rejected because educational reality may be permanently lost.
