# ADR-012

## Title

Observations Reference Teaching Assignments

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Date

2026-07-13

---

## Context

Observations require educational context.

Knowing only the learner is insufficient.

Educational interpretation depends on understanding which educational responsibility produced the observation.

---

## Decision

Observations reference TeachingAssignment rather than only Teacher or Lesson.

---

## Rationale

TeachingAssignment captures the complete educational context in which an observation occurs.

This preserves educational traceability.

---

## Consequences

Observation Engine gains richer educational context.

Future analysis can distinguish between different teaching situations.

---

## Alternatives Considered

### Observation references Teacher only.

Rejected because educational context remains incomplete.
