# ADR-008

## Title

Insights Generate Educational Meaning

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Date

2026-07-14

---

## Context

Once Patterns, Evidence and Confidence had been separated, another question emerged.

Educational knowledge still required interpretation.

This responsibility became the foundation of Insight Theory.

---

## Decision

Insights generate educational meaning.

Insights organize trustworthy educational knowledge into meaningful educational understanding.

Insights never create observations.

Insights never replace educational judgement.

---

## Rationale

Meaning should only emerge after educational awareness has been justified.

Separating interpretation from evidence preserves explainability.

---

## Consequences

Insight becomes the Meaning Layer.

Understanding will synthesize multiple Insights.

Educational decisions remain human responsibilities.

---

## Alternatives Considered

### Insight directly recommends educational action.

Rejected because action belongs to educators.

### Insight replaces professional judgement.

Rejected because SchoolOS is a decision-support system.
