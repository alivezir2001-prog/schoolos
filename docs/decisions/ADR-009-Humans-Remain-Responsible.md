# ADR-009

## Title

Humans Remain Responsible

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Date

2026-07-14

---

## Context

SchoolOS is designed as an explainable educational intelligence system.

As AI capabilities expand, clear boundaries between artificial intelligence and professional educational responsibility become essential.

---

## Decision

Educational responsibility always belongs to people.

Artificial Intelligence may assist observation analysis, pattern recognition, evidence generation, confidence estimation and candidate insights.

Final educational responsibility always remains with educators.

---

## Rationale

Education is fundamentally a human responsibility.

Technology extends professional understanding.

It never replaces professional judgement.

---

## Consequences

No AI component may automatically diagnose learners.

No AI component may automatically determine educational actions.

Every recommendation must remain explainable.

---

## Alternatives Considered

### AI makes educational decisions.

Rejected because educational responsibility cannot be delegated to software.
