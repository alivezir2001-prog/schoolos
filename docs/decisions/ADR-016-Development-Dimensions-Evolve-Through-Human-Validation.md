# ADR-016

## Title

Development Dimensions Evolve Through Human Validation

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Date

2026-07-13

---

## Context

Educational development cannot be completely predefined.

Over time, SchoolOS may discover recurring educational structures that are not represented by existing development dimensions.

---

## Decision

AI may propose Candidate Development Dimensions.

Only human educational experts may formally introduce new Development Dimensions.

---

## Rationale

Educational models should evolve responsibly.

AI assists discovery.

Humans define educational models.

---

## Consequences

Future educational models remain adaptive without losing professional governance.

---

## Alternatives Considered

### AI automatically creates Development Dimensions.

Rejected because educational models require human responsibility.
