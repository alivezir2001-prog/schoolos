# Why SchoolOS Exists

SchoolOS was born from a simple belief:

Technology should not exist to replace people.
It should exist to help them grow.

Schools are not factories.

Students are not raw materials.

Every child is unique.
Every teacher is unique.
Every school has its own culture, strengths and challenges.

SchoolOS exists to make school life easier, support continuous development and help every stakeholder reach their full potential.

---

# Our Mission

To become the digital assistant of the entire school ecosystem by simplifying daily work, supporting continuous development and helping schools make better decisions.

---

# Our Vision

To help schools become better learning communities where every individual has the opportunity to discover their potential, grow continuously and contribute to others.

---

# Our Beliefs

- Every child is a story, not a number.
- Every school is a living community, not merely an institution to be managed.
- Technology exists to empower people, not replace them.
- Growth is more important than comparison.
- Every stakeholder contributes to school success.
- Better decisions create better schools.
