# Evidence Theory

> **Development cannot be measured directly. It can only be understood through meaningful evidence accumulated over time.**

This principle forms one of the philosophical foundations of SchoolOS.

SchoolOS does not attempt to measure people.

Instead, it seeks to understand their development through evidence.

---

# Why Evidence Matters

SchoolOS is built on evidence, not assumptions.

The platform does not define people through isolated observations, scores or subjective opinions.

Instead, it interprets meaningful evidence collected over time.

Every significant insight, recommendation and developmental interpretation must be supported by evidence.

---

# Principle 1 — Development Cannot Be Measured Directly

Development is a complex and lifelong process.

It cannot be observed or measured directly.

Development can only be inferred through meaningful evidence accumulated over time.

SchoolOS therefore never claims to measure development itself.

It interprets evidence related to development.

---

# Principle 2 — An Observation Is Not Evidence

Every observation is valuable.

However, not every observation is evidence.

An observation becomes evidence only when it contributes meaningful information about a person's development.

---

# Principle 3 — Evidence Emerges Through Patterns

Meaningful evidence rarely exists in isolation.

Evidence emerges from coherent patterns observed across time, contexts, observers and experiences.

SchoolOS values patterns over isolated events.

---

# Principle 4 — Evidence Must Be Meaningful

Evidence should help explain development.

A meaningful piece of evidence contributes to understanding how a person is changing and growing.

Not every observable fact is evidence.

Only observations that are relevant to development become evidence.

---

# Principle 5 — Evidence Must Be Corroborated

No important developmental conclusion should rely on a single observation.

Evidence gains strength when multiple independent observations support the same interpretation.

SchoolOS therefore seeks convergence rather than isolated opinions.

---

# Principle 6 — Every Insight Must Be Traceable

Every significant insight generated by SchoolOS must be explainable.

Users should always be able to understand:

- Which evidence supports an insight.
- Why that evidence matters.
- How the interpretation was produced.
- How confident the system is.

Transparency creates trust.

---

# Principle 7 — Evidence Exists to Support Growth

Evidence is never collected to judge people.

Evidence exists to support reflection, understanding and continuous development.

Its purpose is growth.

Not surveillance.
